About TropicalShots

Our Caribbean Story

Founded in 2023 by a team of Caribbean photographers and digital creators, TropicalShots was born from a shared frustration: the lack of authentic, region-specific stock imagery that truly represents the diversity and vibrancy of Caribbean life.

Too often, we saw Caribbean countries represented by generic "tropical" images that failed to capture the unique character of individual islands and their cultures. We set out to change that.

Today, TropicalShots connects businesses, marketers, and creators with high-quality, authentic Caribbean imagery while supporting local photographers across the region.

Our Mission

To provide the most comprehensive collection of authentic Caribbean stock photography while:

  • Accurately representing the diversity of Caribbean cultures
  • Supporting local content creators
  • Making professional Caribbean imagery accessible to all
  • Preserving regional heritage through visual documentation

Why It Matters

Authentic representation matters. Whether you're a:

  • Tourism board promoting your destination
  • Local business creating marketing materials
  • Diaspora member connecting with home
  • Global brand wanting to respectfully feature Caribbean culture

Having access to culturally accurate imagery makes all the difference in how your message is received.
